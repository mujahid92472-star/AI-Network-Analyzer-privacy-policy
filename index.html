Network Vulnerability Scanner with AI-Driven Threat Intelligence ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Target IP Addresses/Hostnames: When you initiate a scan, you enter the IP address or hostname of the network target you wish to analyze.
  • Scan Results: The results of your vulnerability scans are processed and displayed within the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for app functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve user experience.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Financial or payment information

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Performing network vulnerability scans on targets you specify
  • Generating AI-powered threat analysis and recommendations
  • Displaying scan history for your reference
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Scan history is stored locally on your device
  • Server Processing: Scan requests are processed on our secure servers (AWS)
  • Data Retention: Server-side scan data is automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered threat analysis (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • NVD (National Vulnerability Database): For CVE vulnerability data
  • Expo: For app development and updates

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your scan history at any time from within the app
  • Request deletion of any server-side data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Security Disclaimer

Important: This app is designed for authorized security testing only. Users are responsible for ensuring they have proper authorization before scanning any network or system. Unauthorized scanning may be illegal in your jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated effective date.
